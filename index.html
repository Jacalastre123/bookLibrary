Welcome to Book Library

Welcome to Book Library there are 3 things you need to know:

  • Add: This is where you add the book, ISBM support is avaliable whether you want to manually write it or scan it with a barcode scanner.
  • Settings: This is where you can type your name, goal and set a new background colour for the books.
  • Customise: Click on the shelf or the goal meter (filled or unfilled part) to change the colour to your liking. Note that all of the books will be coloured if used on one of them.
